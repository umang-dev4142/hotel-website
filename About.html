Welcome to Hotel Dayal Paradise

Dayal Paradise - a plush address of Comfort, Serenity, Luxury & Greenery, is located in the tranquil backdrops of Vipul Khand, Gomti Nagar, amidst Multiplexes, Recreational Parks, and Corporate & Business Houses, is well connected to the Railway Station, City Bus Terminal & Airport. An ideal abode for Vacations, Conferences, Weddings or any other events, it is complete with excellent facilities for a rewarding stay.
